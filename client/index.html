Drag the animals

A game by Bruno Ferreira

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Hold left mouse button to grab an animal.
  2. Move the animal to your respective shadow.
  3. Release left mouse button to drop the animal.
  4. You score 10 points per hit.
  5. You lose 1 life per mistake.
  6. If you lose all your 3 lifes that over.
